Ultimate AR Coloring

Welcome to Color Color Me

"Color Color Me" is an ultimate AR coloring application.

Augmented reality technology takes your coloring experience to the next level.

How to use

1. Get a sketch by clicking the printer icon in the application or from the following.

2. Color the sketch as you want. You can use any types of pens.

3. Scan it. It's time to play. Corresponding 3D objects will pop up with the colors on it.

Butterfly - Fly to the sky.

Fish - Under the sea.
